## Public Demo

[▶ Watch the 2-minute demo](VIDEO_LINK_HERE)

> *Claude as a system. Not a chat.*
> Workflows, skills, templates and decision systems for non-technical creators.

### What the demo shows

- **The problem** — why every Monday starts with 20 minutes of re-explaining context to AI.
- **The repo structure** — the three layers of the Creator Operating Suite, from outside in.
- **The Creator Entry Skill** — how Claude understands who you are before you ask.
- **PersonalizationOS** — where your voice, decisions and editorial calendar live.
- **A before/after workflow** — same model, different system.
- **How to start** — the one folder to open first.

### How to follow along

1. Open this repo.
2. Read the [starter / Creator Entry Skill](./starter/creator-entry-skill/) — *adjust path if different in your tree*.
3. Try it on a real task you have this week.
4. Open an issue or a discussion with what broke, what surprised you, or what was missing.

### Why a video?

Reading a system is slow. Watching it work is faster.
The demo is **2 minutes, no setup, no plugin, no API** — it shows the transformation, not the architecture.

> Open the repo. Test the starter. Send feedback.
